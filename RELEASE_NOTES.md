## What's new in v4.8.0 — Pets

**New:**
- Pets — roll a companion that buffs your XP while you work
- Feed with cooked food to restore hunger and level up (10 levels)
- Adventures — send your pet on timed expeditions for materials and gold
- Scavenge (Lv3): find 1–3 common materials on 24h cooldown
- Motivation Burst (Lv6): +50% XP for 20 min (3-day cooldown)
- Bond milestones at 7d, 30d, 50 feeds, 100 feeds, Lv10 — each unlocks a reward
- Pet evolution: form changes at Lv10, shown in Bond Celebration modal
- Desktop + in-app notifications for adventure complete and low hunger (<25%)
- Pet drops 1 material per session (5 min min, scales with level)

**UI:**
- Pet tab in nav with hunger and adventure-ready badge indicators
- Mood-based ambient glow behind portrait (lime/happy, orange/playful, red/starving, indigo/sleeping)
- Mood particles — animated effects matching emotional state
- Companion voice lines under pet name
- Bond milestone reward previews before unlock
- Skill buff badge on skills widget showing active bonus (+X%)
- Narrative flavor text in adventure loot modal
- Feed modal empty state navigates to Cooking tab
