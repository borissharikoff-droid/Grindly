🎮 WHAT IS GRINDLY?

Grindly is a desktop companion that gamifies your daily computer usage. It detects your active window, categorizes what you're doing, and rewards you with XP, loot, gold, and achievements — no manual input required.

Started as a productivity tracker. Grew into a full idle RPG with arena combat, farming, cooking, crafting, a player marketplace, guilds, and party raids — all powered by what you actually do on your PC.

🗺️ HOW IT WORKS

1 Launch Grindly — sits in your system tray. No window to keep open.
2 Use your PC normally — code, game, browse, listen to music.
3 Grindly detects your active app and maps it to the right skill category.
4 At session end — earn XP, gold, loot chests, achievement progress.
5 Play the game — fight bosses, farm crops, craft gear, raid with friends.
No browser extension. No manual logging. No timers. Just play your life.
