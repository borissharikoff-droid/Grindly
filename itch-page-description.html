🎮 What is Grindly?

Grindly is a desktop companion that gamifies your daily computer usage. It tracks your active window, categorizes your activity into skills, and rewards you with XP, loot, gold, and achievements — with zero manual input. Just do what you normally do.

It started as a productivity tracker and grew into a full idle RPG: arena combat, farming, cooking, crafting, player marketplace, guilds, and party raids — all powered by what you actually do on your PC.

🗺️ How It Works

1 Launch Grindly — it sits quietly in your system tray. No window to keep open.
2 Use your PC normally — code, browse, game, listen to music, anything.
3 Grindly detects your active app and maps it to a skill category automatically.
4 At session end — you earn XP, gold, loot chests, and achievement progress.
5 Play the game — fight bosses, farm crops, craft gear, raid with friends.

No browser extension. No manual logging. No timers to start and stop. Just play your life.
